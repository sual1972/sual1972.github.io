Why Universal Design

Universal Design is crucial for creating inclusive and accessible web environments, allowing equal access to information and functionalities for all users, regardless of their abilities or disabilities.

Explore the benefits of Universal Design in education, technology, and beyond.

What is Universal Design

Universal Design refers to the design of products and environments to be usable by all people, to the greatest extent possible, without the need for adaptation or specialized design.

Discover the seven principles that form the foundation of Universal Design.

How to Implement Universal Design

To implement Universal Design in web development, start by following the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) and ensuring your website is navigable, understandable, and perceivable by all users.
